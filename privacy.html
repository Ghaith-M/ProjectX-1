Mango Mule

4-5 slices of cucumber 1 oz honey syrup 1.5 oz mango puree 1.5 oz fresh lime juice 1.5 oz ginger beer Ice

Instructions Muddle cucumber and honey syrup in the bottom of a cocktail shaker. Add the mango puree and lime juice and shake with ice vigorously. Strain into a copper mug. Top with ginger beer and stir.

blue lagoon

INGREDIENTS 1 ½ ounces vodka, such as Our/Los Angeles ½ ounce Blue Curaçao ¼ ounce simple syrup ¾ ounce lemon juice DIRECTIONS Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake to chill and combine. Strain into a coupe or cocktail glass.
